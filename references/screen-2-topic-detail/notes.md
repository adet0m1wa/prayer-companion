# Screen 2 — Topic Detail View References

## ref-costar-header.png (Co-Star app)

**What I like:**
- The vertical line separator between header area and body content — clean, editorial feel
- Left-aligned header placement with clear hierarchy (category label → title)
- Generous whitespace around the header section
- Content sections separated with subtle visual breaks

**What I'm NOT taking:**
- The font style, size, or family — defining my own
- The monospaced/astrological metadata text
- The overall light minimal aesthetic — my expanded view has a toned-down version of the card's accent color (80% white, 10% accent)

**How this maps to my Screen 2:**
- Title (e.g. "Love") left-aligned with Rive animated icon to the LEFT of the title
- Vertical line treatment separating header from content body
- Accent color from the card is dialed way back (80% white, ~10% accent) so it's not overwhelming

## ref-bloom-content-cards.png (Bloom app)

**What I like:**
- Card arrangement — thumbnail on left, text content on right, clean rows
- Each card is its own contained unit with clear spacing between cards
- The play icon overlay on thumbnails signals video content
- Duration/metadata aligned to the bottom-right of each card

**What I'm NOT taking:**
- The repeated word typography treatment on thumbnails (Stress Stress Stress)
- The exact card styling — mine will have distinct article vs video treatments
- The bottom nav items

**How this maps to my Screen 2 content:**

### Article cards:
- Thumbnail + title + description
- No play icon
- Metadata row: views, likes, read time (minutes)

### Video cards:
- Thumbnail with LARGER play icon overlay (immediately signals video)
- Title only (no description — keeps it distinct from articles)
- Metadata row: views, likes, duration

### Content type navigation:
- Article / Video toggle aligned to the RIGHT side of the screen
- Right-aligned for right-thumb accessibility
- Sits below the header, above the content cards
